  • Work Related Projects
    • Connecting Hudl Connecting Hudl was a project as part of Hudl's "Mind the Gap" week, a more product/process oriented version of Skunkworks aimed towards fixing gaps in Hudl and the Hudl project. The Connecting Hudl team included Chastity Grant, Mike Jensen, and Austin Wendt and had three parts: a Hudl internal Chrome extension, internal "role" pages, and an internal Wiki. The Chrome extension allowed the user to pick a role ("Developer", "Designer", "QA", etc) and view a small extension window of popular links for that role along with company useful internal links. The extension stored the users role for later visits but still allowed the user to view different pages for different roles. The internal role pages displayed photos for all Hudlies working in that role along with a master list of tools and documentation needed for that role. The internal wiki was an installation of MediaWiki and during Mind the Gap the team created/wrote many company wide reaching articles. Connecting Hudl won the 2015 Mind the Gap "Best in Show" award, chosen by Hudlie vote.
